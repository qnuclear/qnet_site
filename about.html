What Drives Us

Our Values

Every decision we make is guided by three core principles that define QNET's culture and direction.

⚛️

Nuclear First

Every project must teach real nuclear engineering principles. We don't build for the sake of building — we build to understand.

🔬

Interdisciplinary by Design

Nuclear engineering touches every field. Our teams span mechanical, electrical, software, research, and materials — always together.

🚀

Built to Scale

We start with student-scale projects. But every decision we make is with a view toward advanced systems: IEC fusion, plasma, high-voltage.
